# Nikolay Milovidov

A documentary film-maker, an author and director of numerous films and telecasts, a laureate of international festivals. Films were shown on Russian television and on the other countries' channels, including British BBC. Directed more than 20 films and more than 100 TV programmes. Worked as a reporter, an editor, a film director and a stage director.

In my free time I write applications for computers and smartphones that make it easier to manage routine tasks. The main principle is simplicity and reliability.
